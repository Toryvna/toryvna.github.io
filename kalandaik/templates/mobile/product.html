iVOOMi brings to you the i1s smartphone, that's designed to offer you a smooth user experience. The 13.84 cm (5.45 inch) HD display brings you crisp and clear pictures. Overview

Display

Visuals come to life on the 13.84 cm HD display. The 18:9 screen ratio further adds to your viewing experience.

Camera

Capture stunning shots with the dual rear camera system (13 MP + 2 MP). Take beautiful selfies with the 8 MP front camera. You can enhance your pictures using various modes, such as the Bokeh mode, Face Beauty mode and more before uploading them on Facebook or Instagram.

One-touch Fingerprint Sensor

Secure all your private data with the fingerprint sensor. You can also make use of it to call your friends, launch apps, take photos and more conveniently.

Performance

The 1.3 GHz quad-core processor and 3 GB of RAM make multitasking a breeze.

Battery

The 3000 mAh battery keeps the entertainment going on for long. So, enjoy your trips without worrying about your smartphone running out of charge.

Android Nougat 7.0

Powered by the Android Nougat 7.0 OS, this smartphone ensures impressive performance. Enjoy multiple choices and customizations that the world of Android offers.

Connectivity

This smartphone comes with dedicated 4G VoLTE dual SIM slots and microSD card slots. Enjoy seamless connectivity with iVOOMi's 4G VoLTE technology.
